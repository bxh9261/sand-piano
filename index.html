How to use!

The game is a falling sand game, with an added element of audio. Drop sand onto the piano to play notes. Use walls to manipulate the sand and "programmatically" generate music.

You can switch materials with the selector at the top, as well as change the grain size. You can clear the screen by either clicking the "clear" button or changing the grain size.

Future features: Water, which doesn't stack like sand does, and different instruments to play.
